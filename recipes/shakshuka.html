Shakshuka

Ingridients

  • 1 bell paper
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 table spoon of olive oid
  • 3 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • paprika spice
  • ground cummin
  • petersile
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Cut and fry bell paper, red onion and tomato on olive oil.
  2. Add paprika and cummin to get some flavour in it
  3. Crack and add in 3 eggs and let it cook under the cover for 5 minutes
  4. Add some petersile on the top and enjoy!
